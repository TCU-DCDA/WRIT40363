Multimedia Authoring

Master mobile applications and digital publishing through design thinking, user experience principles, and cutting-edge multimedia technologies.

What You'll Master

Develop professional skills in digital media creation, user experience design, and contemporary publishing technologies.

📱

Mobile Applications

Design and develop mobile apps using modern frameworks. Learn UI/UX principles, platform guidelines, and user-centered design methodologies for iOS and Android.

📖

Digital Publishing

Master EPUB standards, interactive e-books, and multimedia integration. Create accessible digital reading experiences with rich media content.

🎨

Design Thinking

Apply human-centered design processes including ideation, prototyping, and iterative testing to create engaging multimedia experiences.

🔬

Self-Directed Learning

Engage with un-grading methodology focused on growth over grades. Develop self-reflection skills and propose your own learning assessments.
