Course Overview

Advanced study in multimedia authoring with emphasis on mobile applications and electronic books. Develop skills in design thinking, user experience, and digital publishing.

📱

Mobile Applications

Learn to design and develop mobile applications using contemporary tools and frameworks. Focus on user interface design, user experience principles, and platform-specific considerations.

📖

Electronic Books

Explore digital publishing technologies and interactive e-book creation. Master EPUB standards, multimedia integration, and accessible design principles for digital reading experiences.

🎨

Design Thinking

Apply human-centered design methodologies to multimedia projects. Learn ideation, prototyping, and iterative design processes for creating engaging digital experiences.

🔬

Self-Reflection Learning

Engage in un-grading methodology focused on learning rather than performance. Write self-reflection essays to demonstrate growth and propose your own assessment.

Course Policies

This course operates on innovative policies designed to maximize learning and creativity

📊

Un-grading System

No traditional grades on assignments. Instead, receive detailed feedback and write self-reflection essays to propose your own grades based on learning progress and growth.

🤖

AI Encouraged

Generative AI (ChatGPT, Gemini, etc.) is permitted and encouraged! Document all AI usage with detailed comments in your projects and reflect on how AI enhances your learning.

📅

Flexible Attendance

No attendance points deducted, but consistent participation is essential for learning progression. Please notify instructor of anticipated absences whenever possible.

⏰

Late Work Policy

No point deductions for late work under our un-grading system. Contact instructor before deadlines for extensions—we're typically happy to provide them with proper communication.
